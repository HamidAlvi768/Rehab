Our Story and Approach to Healing

Founded in 2004 by a team of dedicated healthcare professionals, Musa Medical and Rehab Center was established with a clear mission: to provide compassionate, evidence-based care for individuals struggling with addiction and mental health challenges.

  • Accredited by leading healthcare organizations with recognition for excellence in care.
  • Staffed by a multidisciplinary team of medical professionals, therapists, and recovery specialists.
  • Continuous development of our programs based on the latest research and treatment methodologies.

Our facility spans over 3 acres with modern treatment spaces, comfortable accommodations, and serene outdoor areas designed to foster healing and recovery. We believe that a supportive environment combined with evidence-based treatment is essential for successful recovery outcomes and lasting positive change.
