    A Holistic Approach to Recovery and Healing

    Established in 2004, Musa Medical and Rehab Center has been dedicated to providing compassionate care and evidence-based treatment for individuals struggling with addiction and mental health challenges.

    • Comprehensive assessment and personalized treatment plans for each client.
    • Integration of medical, psychological, and holistic therapeutic approaches.
    • Supportive environment with 24/7 care from experienced professionals.

    Our mission is to empower individuals to overcome addiction, heal from trauma, and develop the skills needed for lasting recovery. We believe in treating the whole person—addressing physical, emotional, social, and spiritual needs—to create a foundation for sustainable wellness and personal growth.

    Our expert team led by Madam Musarat Shabbir, Dr. Attar Munir (Psychiatrist), Dr. Javed Iqbal and Dr. Malik Javed (MBBS), along with our paramedical staff under Nadeem Khan Khattak, ensures the highest quality of care throughout your recovery journey.

    Our Treatment Programs

    Discover our comprehensive range of rehabilitation services designed to support your recovery journey

    Substance Abuse Treatment

    Comprehensive programs for overcoming addiction to alcohol, drugs, and prescription medications.

    • Medically supervised detoxification
    • Individual and group therapy sessions
    • Relapse prevention strategies and aftercare planning

    Mental Health Services

    Specialized treatment for mental health disorders and dual diagnosis conditions.

    • Treatment for depression, anxiety, PTSD, and other disorders

    • Trauma-informed therapy and emotional regulation skills

    Complementary approaches that support overall wellness and recovery.

    • Mindfulness meditation and stress management
    • Art and music therapy for emotional expression
    • Nutritional counseling and physical fitness programs
