Our Treatment Approach

At Musa Medical and Rehab Center, we believe in a comprehensive approach to treatment that addresses all aspects of a person's wellbeing

Assessment

Every client receives a thorough medical and psychological assessment to create a personalized treatment plan.

Individualized Care

Treatment plans are tailored to each person's specific needs, challenges, and recovery goals.

Holistic Healing

We treat the whole person—mind, body, and spirit—not just the addiction or mental health condition.

Continuous Support

Our care extends beyond treatment with comprehensive aftercare planning and ongoing support.

Substance Abuse Treatment

Comprehensive addiction treatment programs that address the physical, psychological, and social aspects of substance use disorders

Medical Detoxification

Our medically supervised detox program helps clients safely manage withdrawal symptoms under the care of Dr. Javed Iqbal and Dr. Malik Javed, our experienced medical professionals.

  • 24/7 medical monitoring
  • Medication-assisted treatment when appropriate
  • Comfortable, private detox facilities

Residential Treatment

Our intensive residential program provides structure, therapy, and community support in a healing environment away from triggers.

  • 30, 60, or 90-day programs available
  • Individual and group therapy sessions
  • Life skills training and relapse prevention

Outpatient Programs

Flexible treatment options that allow clients to maintain work and family responsibilities while receiving the care they need.

  • Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP)
  • Partial Hospitalization Programs (PHP)
  • Evening and weekend sessions available

Mental Health Services

Specialized treatment for mental health disorders and dual diagnosis conditions to support emotional wellbeing and psychological healing

Dual Diagnosis Treatment

Many individuals struggling with addiction also face mental health challenges. Our dual diagnosis program, led by Dr. Attar Munir, our psychiatrist specialist, addresses both conditions simultaneously for more effective recovery.

  • Integrated treatment approaches
  • Specialized therapists trained in both addiction and mental health
  • Personalized medication management

Conditions we treat include:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety disorders
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Post-traumatic stress disorder
  • Personality disorders
  • Schizophrenia

Therapeutic Modalities

We utilize a variety of evidence-based therapeutic approaches tailored to each client's unique needs:

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Identifies and changes negative thought patterns that lead to harmful behaviors and emotional distress.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy

Focuses on emotional regulation, distress tolerance, interpersonal effectiveness, and mindfulness.

Family Therapy

Addresses family dynamics and improves communication to support the recovery process.

Trauma-Informed Therapy

Specialized approaches for healing from past trauma, including EMDR and somatic experiencing.

Holistic Healing Therapies

Complementary approaches that support overall wellness, stress reduction, and personal growth during the recovery journey

Integrating Mind, Body, and Spirit

At Musa Medical and Rehab Center, we believe that true healing encompasses more than just addressing the physical aspects of addiction or mental health disorders. Under the guidance of Madam Musarat Shabbir and our paramedical staff leader Nadeem Khan Khattak, our holistic therapies complement traditional treatment methods by nurturing the whole person.

These evidence-supported complementary approaches help our clients develop healthy coping mechanisms, reduce stress, improve self-awareness, and build resilience—all essential components of long-term recovery.

Mindfulness & Meditation

Daily practice to develop present-moment awareness and reduce anxiety.

Art & Music Therapy

Creative expression to process emotions and experiences non-verbally.

Physical Fitness

Structured exercise programs to improve physical health and mood regulation.

Nutritional Counseling

Education on diet's role in recovery and building healthy eating habits.

Our Levels of Care

Providing a full continuum of treatment options to meet clients where they are in their recovery journey

Our highest level of care provides 24/7 medical supervision during the withdrawal process. Typically lasting 5-10 days, this program ensures clients safely transition through the physical aspects of withdrawal with medical intervention as needed.

Ideal for: Individuals with significant physical dependence on substances requiring medical monitoring.

Our residential program provides a structured environment with 24-hour care and intensive therapeutic interventions. Clients live on-site for 30-90 days while participating in individual therapy, group sessions, and holistic activities.

Ideal for: Those who need removal from their current environment and require intensive support to build recovery skills.

PHP provides intensive treatment 5-7 days per week for 6 hours each day. Clients return home or to a sober living facility each evening. This program offers the intensity of residential treatment with more flexibility.

Ideal for: Individuals transitioning from residential care or those who need intensive treatment but can maintain stability outside treatment hours.

IOP provides treatment 3-5 days per week for 3 hours each day. This program offers flexibility for those who need to maintain work, school, or family responsibilities while receiving treatment.

Ideal for: Clients who have completed higher levels of care or those with milder substance use or mental health issues who have a stable living environment.

Our outpatient services provide therapy and support 1-2 times per week. This can include individual therapy, group sessions, or medication management.

Ideal for: Clients who have completed more intensive levels of care and need ongoing support during their recovery journey.
